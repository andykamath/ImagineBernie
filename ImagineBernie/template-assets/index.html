Who Are We?

ImagineBernie seeks to provide the Sanders campaign additional organized logistical support using social media.

One of our projects is researching for Bernie Connect (Connect.BernieSanders.com) by promoting Connect and collecting survery data across all fifty states.

We help these huge amounts of user data to help our fellow engineers further improve Bernie Connect.

We hope to build upon the ImagineBernie website to include useful features not apparent on Bernie Connect.

There will be more projects to come! Signups to join ImagineBernie will reopen in the near future. Stay tuned!
